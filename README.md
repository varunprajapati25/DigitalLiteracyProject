# Digital Literacy Portfolio
### Student Digital Ambassador Project


## Project Overview
[cite_start]This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio, developed as part of the **CSE0001** course[cite: 2]. [cite_start]As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have curated resources and completed tasks across five modules to demonstrate my ability to navigate the digital world safely, professionally, and effectively[cite: 4, 5, 6].

---

## Module Summaries

### Module 1: Digital Awareness
[cite_start]Created a visual infographic using Canva that covers the definition of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and professional communication to help peers understand the importance of being digitally savvy[cite: 20, 21].

### Module 2: Digital Presence
[cite_start]Established a professional foundational portfolio across **GitHub, LinkedIn, and Stack Overflow**[cite: 29, 33]. [cite_start]This module focused on building a clean, academic identity for future career opportunities[cite: 30, 31].

### Module 3: Platforms & Collaboration
[cite_start]Demonstrated technical proficiency by completing a coding challenge on **HackerRank**[cite: 46, 47]. [cite_start]I also developed a cloud-based collaboration tool using **Google Forms** to quiz peers on their digital knowledge[cite: 51, 52].
* **Take the Quiz:** [https://forms.gle/GtU9vRt7K5Bd7qC29](https://forms.gle/GtU9vRt7K5Bd7qC29)

### Module 4: Communication Etiquette
[cite_start]Developed professional email drafting skills and created a "Social Media Do's and Don'ts" checklist to ensure responsible and effective communication within the academic and professional community[cite: 58, 61, 67].

### Module 5: Cybercrime & Safety
[cite_start]Researched a case study on **UPI/QR Code fraud** and compiled a comprehensive "Stay Safe Online" prevention checklist to help students recognize and avoid common digital threats in India[cite: 73, 77, 82].
