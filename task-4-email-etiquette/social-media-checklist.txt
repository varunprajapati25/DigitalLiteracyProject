5 Do's and 5 Don'ts for responsible social media use as a college student. 

Do’s – 
* Use strong privacy settings on all personal accounts.
* Keep your public bio and profile picture professional for recruiters.
* Fact-check information before sharing or reposting it.
* Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all platforms.
* Use platforms like LinkedIn to follow industry leaders and alumni.
Don’ts – 
* Don't share your real-time location or travel plans publicly.
* Don't post content that could be considered offensive to future employers.
* Don't engage in or encourage cyberbullying or "trolling".
* Don't click on "clickbait" links or suspicious direct messages.
* Don't tag others in photos or posts without their explicit permission.
